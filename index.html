Data Analyst

A Data Analyst is responsible for collecting, processing, and analyzing data to uncover trends and insights that drive business decisions. They work with tools like SQL, Python, Excel, and data visualization platforms such as Tableau or Power BI to transform raw data into meaningful reports. By applying statistical techniques and predictive modeling, they help organizations optimize performance, identify opportunities, and solve complex problems. A strong understanding of databases, data cleaning, and business intelligence is essential for success in this role.
